MINES is a fast-paced, tactical card game for 2–6 players: each turn, draw either a crystal card or a special card to build and upgrade mines, then harvest crystals based on your mine’s level. The first to collect 8 matching crystals wins! With varied strategies, tactical depth, and a dash of luck, MINES stays fresh and keeps you thinking every round.
